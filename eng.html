DIDACTIC MATERIAL ON PLASTIC CARDS

Dear mothers and fathers, grandparents, for the first time in Ukraine, we present to you the didactic material of our own production, made on plastic cards, developed in accordance with the Ukrainian preschool school development program for a preschool child.

Cards from BabyCard are an optimal selection of words, font, size, card thickness, capital letters, funny poems, wonderful drawings.

Cards can be used for both individual and group lessons at home, in classes at a preschool educational institution, in literacy, Ukrainian and English classes, and in elementary school to familiarize children with the outside world.

ABOUT THE BABYCARD PROJECT

WHAT IS THIS PROJECT AND WHY EXACTLY BABYCARD?!

Babycard is the first children's project on plastic cards for children from 3 years old, developed in accordance with the integrated regional program for the development of a preschool child "Ukrainian preschool school".

The material will be useful in kindergarten, elementary school, other preschool educational institutions and at home for studying letters, numbers and familiarizing children with the world around them.

The material is intended for kindergarten teachers, primary school teachers, other institutions and parents.

Cards from Babycard are the optimal choice of words, font, size, card thickness, capital letters, funny poems, wonderful drawings. Cards stimulate interest in classes, help develop fine motor skills, develop speech skills, memory, attention.

SERIES «ENVIRONMENT»

Environment is the world around me. Nature, people, things. The environment (also the natural environment) in the broad sense of the word is space, it is all living and inanimate objects that naturally exist on Earth or in some part of our Motherland - Ukraine, native land.

The environment is the life world that surrounds the child, in which he lives from the first days of his life, which he learns and gives him the opportunity to form the image of his "I", himself as a person.

The environment is the environment of life with which a living being is connected by the metabolism, energy, information, the environment facing the child as an integrity (a holistic picture of the world), in which everything is organically interconnected.

Studying the "Environment", children will get acquainted with the richness of animate and inanimate nature, the diversity of the flora and fauna of their environment, acquire ideas about weather phenomena and their signs, seasonal changes in nature and plant life (trees, bushes, herbs, vegetables, fruits , berries) and animals (animals, birds, fish, insects, wild and domestic), care for their health at different times of the year, receive information about the structure of plants and animals, gain knowledge about the rules of behavior in nature, the need for careful and careful attitude to nature, national customs of celebrating winter and spring holidays.
