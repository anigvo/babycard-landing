SERIES «ENVIRONMENT»

Environment is the world around me. Nature, people, things. The environment (also the natural environment) in the broad sense of the word is space, it is all living and inanimate objects that naturally exist on Earth or in some part of our Motherland - Ukraine, native land.

The environment is the life world that surrounds the child, in which he lives from the first days of his life, which he learns and gives him the opportunity to form the image of his "I", himself as a person.

The environment is the environment of life with which a living being is connected by the metabolism, energy, information, the environment facing the child as an integrity (a holistic picture of the world), in which everything is organically interconnected.

Studying the "Environment", children will get acquainted with the richness of animate and inanimate nature, the diversity of the flora and fauna of their environment, acquire ideas about weather phenomena and their signs, seasonal changes in nature and plant life (trees, bushes, herbs, vegetables, fruits , berries) and animals (animals, birds, fish, insects, wild and domestic), care for their health at different times of the year, receive information about the structure of plants and animals, gain knowledge about the rules of behavior in nature, the need for careful and careful attitude to nature, national customs of celebrating winter and spring holidays.
