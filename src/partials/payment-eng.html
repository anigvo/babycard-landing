PAYMENT

You can choose the method of payment and delivery of goods when placing an order. If you have any questions before downloading the product, please give us a call.

TO THE PRIVATBANK CARD

Transfer of funds through Privat24.

ATTENTION! Only for PrivatBank cardholders.

After placing an order, we will contact you for confirmation. After that, an invoice will be sent to your email.

ATTENTION! When paying the invoice, it is MANDATORY to indicate the order number, your last name and first name!

The invoice must be paid by the person to whom it was issued! Pay the amount including bank commission!

BANK TRANSFER

By transferring funds through Privat24, from a card of any commercial or state bank of Ukraine, from a current account or by depositing cash through a terminal or cash desk of any bank in Ukraine.

After placing an order, we will contact you for confirmation. After that, an invoice will be sent to your email.

ATTENTION! When paying the invoice, it is MANDATORY to indicate the order number, your last name and first name! Pay the amount including bank commission!

TO A NON-CASH ACCOUNT FOR A LEGAL ENTITY

Select the type of payment "Bank transfer", after which we issue you an invoice for payment. After receiving payment, we will send the order at the appointed time to your address.

With non-cash payment, the goods are sold with VAT, with payment to the LLC account.
