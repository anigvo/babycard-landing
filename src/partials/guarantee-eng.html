CLASSIC BABYCARD BANKING PLASTIC CARDS PRODUCTION TECHNOLOGY

The production of children's plastic cards from Babycard is based on the classical banking technology for the production of plastic cards of international payment systems VISA and MasterCard. The sizes of plastic cards correspond to the international ISO standards. All cards are laminated, covered with a layer of transparent plastic laminate. The glossy/matte finish ensures the durability of the cards during use due to sufficient resistance to abrasion and other mechanical influences. Warranty 1 year. Made in Ukraine.
