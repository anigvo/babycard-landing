WHAT YOU NEED TO RECEIVE THE GOODS

To receive goods at the "NOVA POST" branch, you need to have with you:

Number of the consignment note (sent to the client by SMS message to the mobile phone number), original passport or driver's license

Money to pay the cost of transportation services.

If you do not have the opportunity to personally or on time pick up the goods, this can make you a trusted person. This person must have both your passport and his passport with him.

If you are unable to transfer your passport, then you need to contact us and provide the details of the new recipient. Also, contact us if you need to redirect the order to another branch of the carrier in your city.
