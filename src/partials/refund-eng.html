REFUND AND REPLACEMENT OF GOODS

All products presented on the site are subject to exchange in accordance with the Law on the Protection of Consumer Rights. If you are not satisfied with the appearance or the product has visible damage, you have the right to refuse it by writing an application for a return upon receipt of the goods at the branch of the transport and logistics company "NOVA POST".

Goods of good quality that you are not satisfied with for any reason can be returned within 14 days in accordance with the Consumer Rights Protection Law, goods of inadequate quality that have visible damage can be returned only upon receipt. The goods that you took from the office and did not suit you, you send back to us at your own expense according to the tariffs of the transport company "NOVA POST".

If you do not know what to do - do not pick up the shipment, do not sign the express waybill and do not leave the post office - let us know. We will help you resolve the situation.

After the damaged goods are returned to us, we will inspect for the discrepancy or damage indicated by you, and after that we will return the money to you or make a replacement. All goods are insured for the full value of the goods upon shipment, so there will be no problems with the replacement.

Refunds are made within 1-3 business days using the payment method. Only the amount of the goods that came to our account is returned, we do not return the commission for the transfer!

It should be remembered that if you took the goods from the carrier, then claims for mechanical damage or incompleteness of the goods are not accepted!
